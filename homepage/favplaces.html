Some of My Favorite Neighborhood Spots!

Books Are Magic

This is my favorite books store. I've grown up in here, spending hours afterschool, or entire days during summer reading. I've lounged in every corner, on every seating corner in this place. I've explored books from every part of the book store. This place is the best.

Cobble Hill Cinemas

This is the intimate theater in my neighborhood. There are paintings of movies on the outside, as well as both old and new movies playing on the inside. Movie posters cover the walls, and popcorn smell fills the air. I love it there.

Juju's Cafe and Deli

I love getting snacks from one of my local Asian stores. I often pick up sushi burritos or onigiri from here, as well as bubble tea. Their best bubble tea flavor is likely the brown sugar milk tea.
