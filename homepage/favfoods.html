My Favorite Foods

As someone with an incredibly diverse array of ethnicities - I am Moroccan, Filipino, Chinese, Indian, and Spanish - I often use cuisine as a way to connect to my various cultures. Outside of my cultural connections, I generally love being adventurous with my eating, and taking the time to really enjoy the flavors of what I eat.



Moroccan Couscous

One of my absolute favorite foods is my Lola's Moroccan couscous. It's been one of my favorites since I was little. The couscous is juicy, and the flavor of the meat paired with the sweetness of the vegetables, like the carrots, is immaculate.

Chicken Adobo

A classic Filipino dish, chicken adobo, made by my Lolo, is another of my favorite foods. It's incredibly simple to make, if you're making it correctly. Bay leaves, peppercorn, soy sauce, white vinegar, garlic, and chicken, are the recipe for a delicious meal.
